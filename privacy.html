Supporter privacy

Optional accounts and Feed Credits

You do not need an account to create a guest Feed Credit wallet, buy test credits or request a feed. An account is optional and exists to protect your wallet across devices.

What the account stores

The platform stores a managed identity reference, verified email, optional display name, linked-wallet references, session-security records and account-security events. It also shows your existing wallet purchases, reservations, used credits and safely returned credits. Alpacaly does not store your password.

Why it is used

Account data is used only to sign you in, protect and recover your wallet, prevent theft or duplicate ownership, provide your history and support account-security investigations. This implementation does not use account email for marketing.

Your choices

You can remain a guest, download your account data, sign out on all devices or delete your account. Deletion removes account email and display name, revokes sessions and returns linked wallets to new guest recovery credentials. Pseudonymous payment, welfare, feeding and security-audit evidence may need to remain for integrity and legal obligations.

Current status

Supporter accounts, Stripe payments and the feeder are still in local test mode. No managed identity tenant or public service is connected in Phase 8D. A final privacy notice, retention schedule and contact process must be approved before public launch.
